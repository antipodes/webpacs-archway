Type the WORD(S) you want, then press <Enter> or click Submit Search

INNOPAC will search the library's records, looking for the words that you type in various fields,
such as title, contents notes, corporate names, etc.

You may use the Boolean operators AND, OR and NOT
You may also "truncate" words with an asterisk

For example

  • Soviet and Russian
  • Soviet Union or Russia
  • Mexico not New
  • (games or toys) and (child* or infant*)
