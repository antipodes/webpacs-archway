Journal, Magazine, Newspaper Title Search

Click in the box, type the TITLE, then press <Enter> or click Submit Search


Type as much or as little of the EXACT TITLE as you want.
for example:

  • journal of counseling psychology
  • journal of counseling psy

This search engine will find magazines, journals and newspapers which the libraries own.

If your search is unsuccessful, try a TITLE or KEYWORD search.

To find individual periodical articles, use periodical indexes or databases such as Reader's Guide, EbscoHost, etc.
